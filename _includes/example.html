Run Zephyr

    
        $ hadoop fs -mkdir -p /tmp/zephyr-twitter-example/
        $ wget http://sotera.github.io/zephyr/data/data.tsv
        $ hadoop fs -copyFromLocal data.tsv /tmp/zephyr-twitter-example/
        $ cd /opt/zephyr
        $ ./run.sh -job twitter-job.xml
